Wikirace Solver

Developed in ReactJS, TailwindCSS, and Go, this project leverages Breadth First Search (BFS) and Iterative Deepening Search (IDS) algorithms to efficiently determine the minimum number of links required to navigate from one Wikipedia article to another.
